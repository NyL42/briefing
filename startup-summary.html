The problem

Everyone relocating starts every conversation at zero — every time

New city. New agent. New website. New system. The same questions, the same forms, the same frustrating cold starts. Whether you're relocating for a PhD, a job, a family, or just want a better street — nobody has solved this. Until now.

What Locale is

A life-fit matching engine, not a property portal.

Locale combines a conversational AI that understands how you want to live, a neighborhood intelligence layer that scores places across 20+ life signals, a global property marketplace, and a local agent network that receives a full client brief before the first meeting.

The shift

The search starts with life. Properties come second.

Zillow, Rightmove, and Idealista answer "what's available." Locale answers "where do I actually belong?" — then shows you what's available there. For users who just want a house, it works as a great property search too. The depth is opt-in. The intelligence is always on.

How it works

One profile. Travels everywhere.

1

Client talks to the AI

A natural conversation — not a form. The AI asks about lifestyle, priorities, dealbreakers, timeline. This builds a rich, structured profile. It happens once.

2

AI matches neighborhoods, then properties

The platform scores neighborhoods across the target city using 20+ life signals and ranks them by fit. Properties are surfaced inside those matched areas — not the other way around.

3

A local agent receives a full brief

Before any meeting, the matched local agent gets an AI-generated client brief: budget, priorities, dealbreakers, preferred neighborhoods, shortlisted properties. No cold starts. No re-explanation.

4

Profile travels if the client does

Changed destination? New city? Different agent? The profile stays intact. Every new agent gets the same brief. The client never presents themselves again.

Business model

Multiple revenue streams, agent-led growth.

Agent subscription (SaaS)

Local agents pay a monthly fee for access to pre-qualified leads with AI-generated briefs. Higher tier = priority placement.

Listing fees

Property owners and developers pay tiered listing fees for exposure to highly matched, intent-ready buyers and renters.

Referral & API

Mortgage and insurance referral fees. B2B neighborhood data API licensed to developers, city planners, and financial institutions.

Defensible advantage

The data flywheel no competitor can copy fast.

Every conversation improves the matching model. Every agent interaction validates neighborhood data. Every cross-border move adds international signal no local portal captures. The platform gets smarter with every user — and the profile network makes switching cost extremely high for clients who have already invested in their profile.

Geographic focus

Why EU, USA & AU first.

These three regions share the characteristics that make this viable at launch: rich, structured public data on neighborhoods; mature, digitized property markets; large populations making exactly these cross-border moves; and trusted, established agent networks to partner with.

Where we grow next.

Latin America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East follow — as local property data systems mature and cross-border migration corridors grow. The platform architecture is geography-agnostic. The go-to-market is regional by design.
